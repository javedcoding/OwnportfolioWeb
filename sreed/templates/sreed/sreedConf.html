How To Train For Speed Reading

To train yourself use this page every day. Start with a constant word per minute from 100 to 500 (like 200 wpm is the average reader's speed).Train each day with one level of text (starting from level 1). Select how many Words At a Time to read (start from 1 word and go for 3 words eventually). When you feel confortable with current settings increase Words At a Time then Level of Text and finally Words Per Minute
